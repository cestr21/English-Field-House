Who We Are

Welcome to English Field House, where history, flavor, and community unite in a unique culinary journey. Our name honors Amarillo International Airport's rich legacy, beginning with Harold English's English Field in 1929 and evolving into the esteemed Amarillo International Airport in 1976.
‍

  • English Field House blends tradition with innovation, offering a fusion of English hospitality and Mexican flavors. Our menu features a delightful mix of American classics and authentic Mexican breakfast dishes, celebrating culinary diversity.

  • From the savory allure of Chilaquiles, our signature dish, to beloved favorites like fluffy pancakes and hearty omelets, each plate is crafted with care and passion, .

  • English Field House is where cherished memories are made over delicious meals, from breakfast to dinner. Experience our warm hospitality and flavorful dishes as you embark on a culinary journey inspired by Harold English and Amarillo International Airport's legacy.
